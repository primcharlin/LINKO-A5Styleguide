Company Overview

At Linko, we believe that the power of music can bring people together. Our platform connects concert enthusiasts and allows them to find others who share the same passion for live music, artists, and genres! Whether you're swiping through individuals who share similar music interests or exploring concerts forums to meet fellow attendees, Linko makes it easy to find new friends, share experiences, and create lasting memories. We’re here to connect people through their love for music and the magic of live events.
